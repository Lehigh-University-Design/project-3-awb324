Johan Cruyff

About Johan Cruyff

Johan Cruyff was a legendary Dutch football player and coach known for his incredible skills, vision, and influence on the game of soccer. He was born on April 25, 1947, in Amsterdam, Netherlands.

Trophies

Johan Cruyff won numerous trophies throughout his illustrious career, including:

  • 3 European Cups with Ajax (1971, 1972, 1973)
  • 2 La Liga titles with Barcelona (1973-74, 1974-75)
  • 1 Dutch League title with Feyenoord (1983-84)

Hobbies

Outside of football, Johan Cruyff enjoyed:

  • Golfing
  • Spending time with family
  • Traveling
